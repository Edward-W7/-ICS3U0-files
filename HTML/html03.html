Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo is the largest city in Japan, with a population of 13.96 million and an area of 13,500 km2. It combines ultramodern and traditional elements, featuring towering skyscrapers and traditional housing.


The largest cities in Japan are:

  1. Tokyo (population 9,297,000)
  2. Yokohama (population 3,723,000)
  3. Osaka (population 2,694,000)

Japanese facts

Japan is one of the largest East Asian countries, with a population of 125.7 million and a surface area of 377,973 km2. Its most spoken language is Japanese.
Japanese has many interesting facts, including the fact that many of its phrases are actually palindromes.
"かるいきびんなこねこなんびきいるか," which means "How many light clever cats are there?" is the same backwards: "かるいきびんなこねこなんびきいるか"
Japan also has many national attractions, including Mount Fuji and the Imperial Palace.
