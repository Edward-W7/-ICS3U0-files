Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo is the largest city in Japan, and has a population of 13.96 million, and an area of 13,500 km2.
It mixes ultramodern and traditional, with towering skyscrapers and traditional housing.
The largest cities in Japan are:

  1. Tokyo (population 9,297,000)
  2. Yokohama (population 3,723,000)
  3. Osaka (population 2,694,000)
